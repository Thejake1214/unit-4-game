Crystal Collector

Game Instructions


  1. The number you want to score will be displayed on the screen as"Target Number"
  2. The crystals are pictured below.
  3. When you click on a crystal you will add a random number of points to your score.
  4. You win the game by achieving the total score named "Target Number".
  5. You lose if your total score goes over the target number.
  6. You won't know the number of each crystal until you click on it.
  7. The numbers of each crystal will change every time the game is restarted.
